// GENERAL

BASE82 is a tactical game discovery platform — a hub for tracking, rating, and discovering games. Think of it as mission control for your gaming life.
Yes. BASE82 is completely free. Create an account, browse the database, view leaderboards, and track your stats — no subscriptions, no paywalls.
BASE82 covers all major platforms — PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch, and mobile. Our database is powered by RAWG.io which indexes 500,000+ games across every major platform.

// ACCOUNT

Hit SIGN UP from the login page. Enter your username, email, and a password. You can also use Google, Steam, Discord, or Facebook to authenticate instantly.
Click "FORGOT?" on the login page and enter your email. A recovery link will be sent within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it doesn't arrive.
Username changes are available once every 30 days from your profile settings. Choose carefully — your tag shows on the leaderboard.

// FEATURES

The weekly leaderboard is determined by a combination of RAWG community rating, number of additions that week, and platform activity. It resets every Monday at 00:00 UTC.
